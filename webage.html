Welcome to Kethel Chicken

About Us

Kethel Chicken is a new and exciting fast-food destination for everyone with a craving for juicy, tender and flavorful chicken dishes. Our menu features a variety of delicious offerings such as crispy and crunchy-fried chicken, juicy burgers, mouth-watering sandwiches, and spicy wings. Whether you are looking for a quick and delicious meal or a fun and casual dining experience, Kethel Chicken is the perfect place to satisfy your hunger. With our friendly and welcoming atmosphere and our fast and efficient service, you can count on Kethel Chicken to provide a dining experience that you will not soon forget. So come on down to Kethel Chicken, where "goodness tastes!"

Best Sellers

  • Veg Burgers

    Welcome to our Restarunt, where you can find the most delicious and creative veggie burgers with the crispiest and tastiest French fries! Our veggie burger is made with a unique, house-made blend of vegetables and beans, and it comes accompanied with your choice of toppings and condiments. If you think this would be enough, you haven’t heard about our French fries! They are twice-fried to perfection and they come with our special seasoning mix for an extra flavor blast. Our French fries can be eaten as a side dish or on their own, but when combined with our veggie burgers, they are a match made in heaven! Come on in and sample our vegetarian delight yourself, but be warned, once you’ve tried it, you will want to come back for more!

  • Lemondade

    Our delicious, refreshing lemonade is the perfect drink to quench your thirst on a hot summer day! Our lemonade is made fresh daily with real lemons, filtered water and just the right amount of sugar, ensuring each and every glass is perfectly balanced in terms of sweetness and tanginess. You can order your lemonade with or without ice, and we offer a variety of different garnishes such as lemon slices, fresh mint leaves and maraschino cherries, so you can customize your lemonade to your heart's desire. Come and try our lemonade today, you won't regret it!

  • Roti Meal

    The smell of the spicy and savory roti meal with potato Gravy, carrot slices, roti, and spices fills the air. You can't help but drool as you imagine taking your first bite into the soft and flaky roti, the tender and flavorful potato gravy, and the sweet and crunchy carrot slices. The spices blend together perfectly to create a mouthwatering and satisfying dish. It's time to dig in!
